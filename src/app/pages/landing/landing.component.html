Understanding the world’s knowledge production on Grassland This project showcases the results of a comprehensive scientometric analysis of global scientific publications focused on grasslands. The analysis aimed to:
  • Identify key research groups and institutions specializing in various themes related to grasslands
  • Highlight potential data gaps in the existing research
Explore Further This website offers several ways to explore the analysis results.
Database Access: View the complete dataset of analyzed publications.
Publication Clusters: Investigate the identified clusters of related publications.
Results & Analysis: See graphs, charts, and statistical analyses summarizing the findings.
Data Source
The literature used for this analysis was obtained from the OpenAlex database, a comprehensive open-access repository of scientific publications.
Priem, J., Piwowar, H., & Orr, R. (2022). OpenAlex: A fully-open index of scholarly works, authors, venues, institutions, and concepts. ArXiv. https://arxiv.org/abs/2205.01833
